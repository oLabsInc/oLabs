Reference 1 SpreadShield

The scene opens pitch black, inside an empty warehouse with the camera pointed at the a Lamborghini in the center. Slowly, 1 by 1, overhead lights turn on (the ones that make a loudish noise when they switch on), exposing the scene little by little by removing the darkness. The camera (which acts a persons POV) slowly makes it's way from in front of the Lamborghini, as if slowly walking up to the drivers door. The camera slowly proceeds to take the position of the person driving the Lamborghini, as if the person were to get in and sit down in the drivers seat. The garage door of the warehouse begins to slowly open, exposing the city street. And then the beat drops in the background audio.

As the beat drops, the WideSpread logo will appear in the middle of the windshield. as a HUD element.The windshield will now start to function a little more showcasing small basic subtle features as the Lamborghini slowly approaches the garage door to exit the warehosue.

The Lamborghini exits and enters onto the city street by taking a right. As the car turns, A few map markers will move across the windshield entering from the right side and moving towards the left until they line up the buildings that they represent. Also showing the cars current speed, current desination/navigation step (turn right arrow), as it mirrors the same windshield UI on the connected SpreadShield interactions touchscreen.

The car starts speeding up and moving forward down the street, with new map markers appearing on the windshield for new buildings as they come closer to the car.

A phone will ring a couple of times, and we'll finally answer the call. A video chat feed will appear in the center of the windshield with the incoming video call stream.

Now we'll just drive around aimlessly showcasing features. Listed below are the core features of SpreadShield (any feature on WideSpread is available on SpreadShield, so we can ignore most of these, we only want the driving features for SpreadShield, but some random features on WideSpread will be good for filler content that still demonstrates SpreadShields functionality).

  • Pedestrian Detection Alert
  • Object Detection (Road obstructions only)
  • Music Playlists & Media Player
  • Movie/Show/Video Streaming (small & large)
  • Contacts List
  • Navigation
  • Hand Signal Detection for construction/emergency traffic hand signals
  • Traffic Alerts
  • SocialSpread Notifications
  • Nearby Activity
  • Nearby Information
  • IoT Device Control System
  • Current Location & Destination Details Sharing
  • Cora & Cory Assisstive Help
  • 360⚬ Dashcam Recording
  • Passenger Gaming
  • Current Astral Map in view of windshield. Same as Places overlay, just with stars and planets.
  • I'll add more after I look at notes. These were off the top of my head.
